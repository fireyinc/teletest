Welcome to TeleTest!

TeleTest is a site which tries to recreate the classic teletext look, but with more modern technology. No telly required either!


Teletest pages can be requested to be added. Art for things like buttons have to look like something straight out of Teletext.

  • No more than 7 colors
  • Cannot be HD

Pages will be numbered depending upon order of addition. The first page would be 001, next would be 002, and so on.
